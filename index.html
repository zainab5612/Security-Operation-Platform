Act 2 — Project Boundaries

In-Scope vs. Out-of-Scope (Phase 1)

Disciplined operational boundaries protecting the deliverable timeline.

In Scope for Phase 1

  • Google Workspace log ingestion & historical import
  • Log normalization & searchable event repository
  • Workspace Security Scorecard & Identity Rules
  • Rule-based threat detection & basic event correlation
  • Explainable risk scoring & Incident Management
  • KPI dashboard, MITRE ATT&CK & NIST CSF tracking
  • Analyst-reviewed AI assistance & working prototype

Out of Scope for Phase 1

  • Fully automated account suspension / containment
  • Machine learning detection algorithms at launch
  • Full endpoint agent or network packet inspection
  • Replacement of Google Workspace, AWS, or Orkavault
  • Full enterprise SIEM feature parity
  • 24/7 manned SOC coverage
  • Production deployment prior to security review
